RowChat Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 18, 2025
Support Contact: witchnock@gmail.com
Version: 1.0

This Privacy Policy explains how RowChat ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Username
  • Email address
  • Password (stored as encrypted hash)
  • Profile picture/avatar (if uploaded)
  • Account creation date

Content You Create

  • Messages sent in public and private rooms
  • Direct messages
  • Files and media you upload
  • Room descriptions and settings
  • Friend requests and connections

Automatically Collected Information

  • IP address
  • Device information (browser type, operating system)
  • Usage data (pages visited, features used, time spent)
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies
  • Last seen timestamps
  • Online/offline status

Optional Information

  • Bio/profile description
  • Status messages
  • Custom preferences and settings

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide the Service: Enable account creation, messaging, and all RowChat features
  • Safety and Security: Detect and prevent abuse, spam, harassment, and violations of our Terms
  • Moderation: Apply automated and manual content filters
  • Communication: Send important updates about the service
  • Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to improve features
  • Legal Compliance: Respond to legal requests and prevent illegal activity

3. How We Share Your Information

Public Information

The following information is visible to other RowChat users:

  • Username
  • Avatar
  • Bio and status messages
  • Messages in public rooms
  • Online status (to friends)
  • Friend list (to friends)

We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

We May Share Information With:

  • Service Providers: Third-party services that help us operate (hosting, analytics, email delivery)
  • Law Enforcement: When required by law, subpoena, or court order, or to prevent imminent harm
  • Safety Partners: Organizations like NCMEC when we detect child exploitation
  • Business Transfers: If RowChat is acquired or merged, your data may transfer to the new entity

4. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data retained while your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Most data deleted within 90 days, some retained for legal/safety reasons
  • Messages: Stored indefinitely unless manually deleted
  • Logs: Security and usage logs retained for up to 1 year
  • Backups: May persist in backups for up to 30 days after deletion

5. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Opt-Out: Decline non-essential data collection
  • Object: Object to certain data processing

To exercise these rights, contact witchnock@gmail.com with your request.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep you logged in
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze how you use RowChat
  • Improve performance

You can disable cookies in your browser, but this may limit functionality.

Types of Cookies We Use:

  • Essential: Required for the service to work
  • Analytics: Help us understand usage patterns
  • Preferences: Remember your settings

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures including:

  • Encrypted password storage (bcrypt hashing)
  • HTTPS encryption for data in transit
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security audits
  • Automated threat detection

However, no system is 100% secure. You use RowChat at your own risk.

8. Children's Privacy

RowChat requires users to be at least 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, please contact witchnock@gmail.com immediately and we will delete the account.

Users ages 13-17 should have parental permission before using RowChat.

9. International Data Transfers

RowChat is hosted in the United States. If you access RowChat from outside the US, your information will be transferred to and processed in the US, which may have different data protection laws than your country.

10. Third-Party Links

RowChat may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites. Please review their privacy policies before sharing information.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Effective Date." Material changes will be announced via the service.

Your continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.

12. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information
  • Right to deletion of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

We do not sell your personal information.

To exercise your CCPA rights, contact witchnock@gmail.com.

13. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have rights under GDPR including:

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent

Legal Basis for Processing:

  • Contract performance (providing the service)
  • Legitimate interests (safety, security, improvement)
  • Legal compliance
  • Your consent (where applicable)

To exercise your GDPR rights, contact witchnock@gmail.com.

14. Do Not Track

Some browsers have "Do Not Track" features. RowChat does not currently respond to DNT signals.

15. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you via email within 72 hours of discovering the breach, as required by law.

16. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy or your data:

  • Email: witchnock@gmail.com
  • Response Time: We aim to respond within 48 hours
By using RowChat, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.
